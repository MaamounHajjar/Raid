## 🎮 Raid - User Guide

Raid is a 2D village-defense game. The goal is to survive enemy waves and protect the villagers for as long as possible. During the game, the player must move around the map, fight enemies, trade with villagers, and use potions at the right time.

### Main Objective
The main objective is to survive all enemy waves while keeping the village alive.
The player loses if:
* the player dies
* all villagers die

The player wins if:
* all waves are completed
* no enemies remain on the map

### How the Game Starts
When the game opens, a start screen appears with the controls and basic instructions.
Press ENTER to start the game.

### Controls
* W, A, S, D to move
* 1 to equip the sword
* 2 to equip the bow
* F to attack or shoot
* 3 to select the healing potion
* 4 to select the strength potion
* Y to consume the selected potion
* E near a villager to trade
* H to buy a healing potion
* C to buy a strength potion
* ESC to pause the game

### Weapons
The player can use two weapons:

**Sword**
* close-range weapon
* useful when enemies are near
* deals direct damage in melee combat

**Bow**
* ranged weapon
* useful for attacking enemies from a safer distance
* allows the player to fight before enemies get too close

The player can switch between weapons at any time using 1 and 2.

### Potions
Potions help the player survive and fight better.

**Healing Potion**
* restores health
* useful after taking damage

**Strength Potion**
* increases attack power for a limited time
* useful during difficult waves or against stronger enemies

To use a potion:
1. select it with 3 or 4
2. press Y to consume it

### Trading
Villagers are not only part of the village, but also useful for survival.
When the player is close to a villager, pressing E opens trading.
The player can buy potions by spending emeralds:
* H buys a healing potion
* C buys a strength potion

Trading is important because potions can help a lot during later waves.

### Enemies
The game includes different enemy types.

**Illager**
* regular humanoid enemy
* chases targets and attacks directly
* less dangerous than stronger ravager, but still a threat in groups

**Ravager**
* stronger enemy
* has more health and higher damage
* more dangerous during later waves

As the game continues, more enemies appear and the waves become harder.

### Villagers
Villagers are important because they are part of the objective.
They move around the village and try to stay alive, but enemies can attack them.
If all villagers die, the game is lost.
Because of this, the player should not only focus on personal survival, but also defend the village.

### Map and Environment
The game world contains different types of terrain.

**Grass**
* normal terrain
* movement is normal

**Water**
* slows movement
* use to your advantage

**Lava**
* causes damage
* dangerous to stand on, kills almost instantly

**Cobblestone, Oak Wood/Planks, Doors and Trees**
* non-walkable blocks
* cannot be penetrated by arrows

So the map is not only decoration. It affects movement and combat decisions.

### Wave Progression
The game is divided into waves.
Each new wave brings more enemies and more danger.
This means the player must:
* choose weapons carefully
* use potions at the right moment
* protect villagers
* manage space and movement on the map

### What to Expect While Playing
At the beginning, the game is easier and helps the player learn the controls.
Later, the pressure increases because enemies become more numerous and stronger. The game is not only about attacking, but also about timing, positioning, and protecting the village.

### Final Advice
To play well:
* keep moving
* do not waste potions
* switch weapons depending on the situation
* use trading when possible
* protect villagers, not only yourself
